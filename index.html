Apr 2026 with Prof. Ramij Rahaman

Comparative Study of BVP Solvers: PINN vs FDM vs Analytical

Compared analytical, finite-difference (FDM) and physics-informed neural network (PINN) methods across linear, nonlinear (Bratu) and inverse boundary value problems. Implemented FDM with Newton iteration for the nonlinear case and PINNs in PyTorch with automatic differentiation, hard/soft boundary enforcement and Adam + L-BFGS training. Recovered an unknown coefficient from noisy observations by treating it as a trainable variable optimized jointly with the network, which FDM and analytical methods cannot do, and verified second-order FDM convergence empirically.

Apr 2026 NQFO 2026 contest entry

Implied Volatility Surface Completion

A two-layer ensemble (quadratic smile fit + LightGBM) predicting 1,699 missing implied-volatility entries on a synthetic NIFTY50 options surface, anchoring the smile fit on same-day observed IVs to recast the task as within-date interpolation rather than cross-date prediction. No-arbitrage built in: calendar-spread monotonicity via isotonic regression on total variance, butterfly positivity via a convexity constraint on the smile fit; put-call parity left unenforced after EDA confirmed genuine skew rather than noise. K-means regime detection (calm / normal / turbulent) on ATM 1M IV and term-structure slope fed LightGBM, with blend weights tuned per moneyness bucket by leave-one-out validation.

Apr–May 2025 with Prof. Kaushik Jana

E-commerce Warehouse Location Optimization

Formulated warehouse placement as a facility location problem and solved it as a MILP in Python (PuLP + CBC) across India’s top 10 e-commerce cities. Modeled binary open/assign decisions under budget, warehouse-count, delivery-time and single-assignment constraints, with Haversine great-circle distances driving the delivery-cost objective. Selected 4 of 10 candidate cities as regional hubs (west / north / south / east) covering all demand centres within the constraints, with solver optimality validated against the model formulation.
